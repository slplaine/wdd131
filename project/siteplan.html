Crochet Hobby

A website introducing crochet as a creative and relaxing hobby.

Site Name

Crochet Hobby – This name represents a website dedicated to crochet as a relaxing and creative pastime. I chose it because crochet is my personal hobby and I want to share it with others.

Optional domain: crochet-hobby.org

Site Purpose

The site introduces crochet as a hobby, explains basic tools and materials, and provides beginner tips. It will also showcase common crochet projects and highlight why people enjoy crocheting.

Scenarios

  • What basic tools do I need to start crocheting?
  • Where can I find simple crochet project ideas for beginners?

Color Schema

Primary Color:#3D5A40 (Olive Green) – used for headings and main elements, bringing natural elegance.

Accent Color:#E07A5F (Terracotta) – applied to buttons, highlights, and decorative details, adding warmth and an artisanal touch.

Background Color: #F4E1D2 (Light Sand)– used as the main background, creating a soft and cozy atmosphere.

Text Color: #2C2C2C (Charcoal Gray) – used for body text, ensuring readability and contrast.

White Color: #FFFFFF – used for clean sections and breathing space.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display – elegant and classic, used for titles and headings to add sophistication.

Body Font: Roboto – clean and highly readable, used for paragraphs and general content to ensure clarity.

Wireframes

The following wireframes describe the layout of the home page in mobile and desktop views. They are simple representations of the structure and relationship of content.

Mobile View

In the mobile layout, the logo appears at the top, followed by the site title centered below it. The tagline is placed directly under the title. Content sections are stacked vertically in a single column for easy scrolling. The footer is placed at the bottom with simple centered text.

Desktop View

In the desktop layout, the logo is larger and aligned next to the site title in the header. The title is more prominent and centered, with the tagline directly below. The main content is organized into two columns, allowing text and images to be displayed side by side. The footer spans the full width of the page, providing balance to the layout
